Customer: Alright, that makes sense. Is there any way to check how much data I've used so far this month? 

Agent: Yes! You can easily check your data usage from your account dashboard. You can also sign up for text or email notifications that will alert you when you're nearing your data limit for the month so you can adjust your usage accordingly.
